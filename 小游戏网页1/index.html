About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face endless waves of terrifying monsters. Upgrade your hero, unlock powerful abilities, and compete for the highest score. Whether you're on PC or mobile, enjoy smooth gameplay and intuitive controls. Can you become the ultimate survivor?

Game Features

  • Fast-paced survival action with increasing difficulty
  • Multiple heroes and upgrades to unlock
  • Global leaderboards and achievements
  • Seamless play on desktop and mobile devices
  • No download required – play instantly in your browser

How to Play

  1. Use your keyboard or touch controls to move your hero.
  2. Defeat monsters and collect power-ups.
  3. Upgrade your abilities between waves.
  4. Survive as long as possible to set a new high score!
