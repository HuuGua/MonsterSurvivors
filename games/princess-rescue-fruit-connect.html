Princess Rescue Fruit Connect
Princess Rescue Fruit Connect is a delightful and colorful puzzle game where you help the princess rescue her friends by connecting matching fruits! Solve hundreds of fun and challenging levels, use boosters to overcome tricky puzzles, and enjoy beautiful graphics and relaxing music. Perfect for all ages, this game will test your logic and observation skills while providing hours of entertainment.

How to Play
  1. Connect matching fruits by drawing lines between them.
  2. Clear all fruits before time runs out or moves are used up.
  3. Use boosters to solve difficult puzzles.
  4. Rescue the princess's friends and unlock new levels!
